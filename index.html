My Software Development Goals

Year 1

  • Learn JavaScript and React: Become proficient in building responsive, scalable, and maintainable front-end applications using JavaScript and React.
  • Build 5 personal projects: Develop and deploy five personal projects on platforms like GitHub or GitLab to demonstrate skills and showcase creativity.
  • Attend 2 tech conferences: Attend at least two conferences or meetups to network with industry professionals, learn about industry trends, and gain insights from experts.
  • Improve problem-solving skills: Practice solving problems on sofware communities to enhance my problem-solving skills and coding efficiency.
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends: Regularly read industry blogs, articles, and books to stay informed about the latest technologies, frameworks, and best practices.

Year 2

  • Launch a startup or join a tech team: Either launch a startup, join a tech team, or contribute to open-source projects to apply skills in real-world scenarios.
  • Speak at a tech conference: Share knowledge and experiences by speaking at a tech conference, meetup, or webinar.
  • Develop expertise in a specific domain: Specialize in a specific area like cybersecurity, data science, or cloud computing to stand out in the industry.
  • Mentor junior developers: Share knowledge and experience with junior developers, either through formal mentorship programs or informal guidance.
